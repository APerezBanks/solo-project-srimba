Visit Torshov

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant
district close to the center of Oslo.

Top three activities to do at Torshov

Attend a show or a concert

The Torsov theater is a great place to spend your
night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park

The Torashov Park is perfect for a piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Lilleborg field

Play volleyball, football, ball or lift some weight.
